Psychology safety boundaries

GENEVIEVE HEALTH supports operational continuity, supervision, reception, staff safety and evidence. It does not diagnose, assess or treat; calculate suicide risk; declare a person safe or unsafe; replace a psychologist; replace emergency services; or automatically contact police, family or emergency contacts.

All urgent workflows require approved Mood & Mind Centre procedures and authorised human decisions. Call 000 in an immediate emergency.
