# Irene — Practice Director and Clinical Psychologist

## Controlled demonstration authority

Irene’s default role is Practice Director. The demonstration gives her a full fictional-data operational view while preserving human clinical judgement and minimum-necessary access for other roles.

| Area | Irene can do | May be delegated | Human approval retained |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Daily command oversight | Review practice state, urgent and overdue work, owners, due times and evidence | Queue review and routine follow-up | Escalation and closure rules |
| Emergency cover | Activate cover, name first and second backup, review acceptance, contact outcomes and return handover | Contact coordination and evidence collection | Final review and closure |
| Staff and coverage | Change availability, arrange coverage, review breaks, workload, lone work, admin buffer, debrief and support | Routine roster and support actions | Coverage eligibility and unresolved escalation |
| Provisional supervision | Approve supervisors, scope, evidence, actions and progression | Scheduling and evidence collection | Competency and scope decisions |
| Client continuity | Review missed appointments, failed contact, no next appointment, consent, transition and accepted backup | Approved contact attempts | Clinical action and accountable closure |
| Reception | Approve scripts, access boundaries, callback ownership and transfer pathways | Reception actions within scope | Clinical concern decisions |
| Safety and incidents | Assign authorised psychologists, review incidents, staff support and follow-up | Facts and evidence collection | Human clinical and manager decisions |
| WHS and insurance | Review hazards, worker-event dates, controls, policy evidence and reminders | Register maintenance | Manager and professional review |
| Daily checks and closure | Approve opening, midday, end-of-day and practice-closure checks | Checklist completion | Unresolved red/black matter review |
| Documents and forms | View letters, proposal, workflow maps, governance records and working forms | Approved operational file use | Access and retention rules |
| Reports and audit | Export fictional continuity, supervision, workforce, incident, compliance and audit records | Report preparation | Final interpretation and sign-off |
| Governance and refinement | Approve terms, roles, scripts, timers, policies, permissions and readiness gates | Drafting and configuration support | Practice approval before live use |

Routine operational work may be delegated to authorised staff. The demonstration does not diagnose, calculate a clinical risk score, make an automatic contact decision or replace Mood & Mind Centre’s approved procedures.
