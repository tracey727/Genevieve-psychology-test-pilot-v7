# Mood & Mind Centre official demo script

## Opening
“GENEVIEVE HEALTH is not replacing the clinical record or the psychologist. She is the operational safety layer that makes responsibilities, coverage, supervision and unfinished actions difficult to lose.”

## 1. Command Centre
Show the whole-practice colour state, urgent responsibilities, staff availability, continuity flags, open safety checks and supervision items.

## 2. Staff absence
Run **Staff absence**. Show how affected client responsibilities receive a named eligible backup and remain red until accepted.

## 3. Reception and client safety
Run **Distressed call**. Explain that reception follows an approved script and transfers to an authorised psychologist; the program does not score risk.

## 4. Provisional psychologist support
Run **Supervision gap**. Show supervisor allocation, scope evidence, overdue supervision, actions and the evidence gate preventing a false green state.

## 5. Staff WHS, WorkCover and insurance
Run **WHS incident**. Show aggression/psychosocial hazards, staff support, reporting review, insurance registers and audit evidence.

## 6. Close with evidence
Open Tasks & Handovers. Add evidence, advance the workflow and show that closure requires an outcome and all required evidence.

## Final statement
“This is a controlled fictional-data demonstration. Mood & Mind Centre will approve the clinical, privacy, WHS, insurance and emergency rules before any production or real-data use.”
