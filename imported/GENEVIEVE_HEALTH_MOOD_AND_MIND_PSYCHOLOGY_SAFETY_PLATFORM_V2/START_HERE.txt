GENEVIEVE HEALTH™ — MOOD & MIND CENTRE
PSYCHOLOGY SAFETY PLATFORM V2
Build: MIND-2.0.0

1. Extract the ZIP completely.
2. Open index.html for a quick Windows demonstration.
3. For the most reliable local test, open a terminal in this folder and run:
      python -m http.server 8080
   Then open http://localhost:8080
4. Keep all data fictional. Do not enter real client or worker information.
5. Use the Demo role selector to show Irene, clinical lead, psychologist,
   provisional psychologist, reception and read-only audit views.
6. Use the four Official demo controls on the Command Centre to demonstrate:
   staff absence, distressed caller, provisional supervision gap and WHS incident.

THIS V2 REMOVES NON-PSYCHOLOGY CARE WORKFLOWS.
It replaces them with:
- clinical psychology staff and capability records;
- provisional psychologist supervision and scope gates;
- client continuity and accepted coverage;
- reception scripts and clinician escalation;
- psychology-practice safety checks;
- psychosocial hazard, aggression and lone-work controls;
- WorkCover reporting reminders and restricted worker-event records;
- workers compensation, professional indemnity and business insurance registers;
- audit reports and evidence-gated closure.

CONTROLLED DEMO ONLY
This is deployable as a fictional-data demonstration. It is not yet a production
clinical system. See README.md and docs/PRODUCTION_READINESS_CHECKLIST.md.
