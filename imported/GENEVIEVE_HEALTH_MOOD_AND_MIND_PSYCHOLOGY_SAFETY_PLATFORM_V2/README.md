# GENEVIEVE HEALTH™ — Mood & Mind Psychology Safety Platform V2

A working, deployable **controlled demonstration** for a psychology practice. It is designed around Mood & Mind Centre, Irene’s role as Practice Director and Clinical Psychologist, qualified psychologists, provisional psychologists, reception staff and clients.

## What changed from V1

Non-psychology care workflows were removed. The daily checks are now psychology-practice operational checks covering staff attendance, accepted coverage, reception escalation, telehealth arrangements, workload, breaks, lone work, unresolved callbacks and end-of-day handover.

## Working modules

- Psychology Practice Command Centre with green/yellow/amber/red/black operational state.
- Appointments and telehealth preparation.
- Staff registration prompts, capability/scope, workload, leave and accepted coverage.
- Provisional psychologist supervision, supervisor access, agreements, evidence and progression.
- Client continuity, non-attendance, failed contact, consent, accessibility and external handover.
- Reception callbacks, approved placeholder scripts and immediate clinician transfer.
- Referral and waitlist evidence gates.
- Tasks, accepted handovers, timed escalation and evidence-gated closure.
- Immediate Safety Concern workflow with strict human-decision boundaries.
- Staff, privacy, aggression and near-miss incident management.
- Psychology-practice opening, midday and closing safety checks.
- WHS psychosocial hazard register, WorkCover reporting reminders and restricted worker-event register.
- Workers compensation, professional indemnity, public liability, cyber and business-continuity insurance register.
- Staff training, policy approval, reports, JSON exports and audit CSV.
- Six role views and an offline-capable PWA shell.

## Critical boundary

The package is a static fictional-data demonstration. It has no secure production authentication, encrypted database, clinical-system integration or approved emergency integration. Do not enter real information until the production safeguards and Mood & Mind Centre governance are approved.
