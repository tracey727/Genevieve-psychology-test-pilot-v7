# Audit readiness register

The demo can organise the following fictional evidence for management or adviser review:

## Psychology workforce
- role and registration-status prompt;
- provisional/general registration category;
- Board-approved supervisor status prompt;
- scope and capability tags;
- professional indemnity status and review/expiry date;
- workload, high-support load, breaks, leave and backup;
- training and policy acknowledgement.

## Provisional supervision
- provisional and supervisor;
- agreement/scope evidence;
- date, format and duration;
- focus areas;
- attendance, actions and acknowledgements;
- competency-progress statement made by the supervisor;
- overdue supervision escalation.

## WHS and worker support
- psychosocial and physical hazard register;
- people exposed, controls, owner, review date and residual state;
- violence/aggression and near-miss incidents;
- staff check-in, debrief, supervision and workload actions;
- lone/remote work controls;
- restricted worker-event date and reporting status;
- WorkCover review reminder and claim-consent field.

## Insurance
- workers compensation accident insurance;
- professional indemnity register;
- public liability;
- cyber/privacy cover;
- business interruption/property cover;
- policy reference, holder, expiry, verification and evidence placeholder.

## Client and practice operations
- consent and communication permissions;
- accepted coverage;
- referrals and missing evidence;
- appointments, non-attendance and follow-up;
- reception callbacks and scripts;
- incidents, actions, outcome, review and closure;
- append-only demonstration audit export.

This register assists preparation. It does not certify legal, regulatory, insurer or professional compliance.
