# Psychology-only scope and V2 removals

V2 is configured only for a private psychology practice.

Removed from the user-facing program:
- non-psychology care-round language;
- resident-care and accommodation workflows;
- medical ward, bed, medication-round and discharge workflows;
- animal-care terminology and kennel/cattery workflows.

Retained only as general GENEVIEVE architecture:
- clear colour states;
- named ownership and backup;
- evidence-gated completion;
- accepted handovers;
- time-stamped checks and incident records;
- escalation when work is overdue, declined or unowned.

Psychology-specific replacements:
- qualified psychologist and provisional psychologist roles;
- Board-approved supervisor and supervision-plan records;
- scope, capability and professional indemnity prompts;
- staff workload, emotional demands, fatigue and vicarious-trauma controls;
- non-attendance, failed contact and client continuity;
- reception boundaries and rapid authorised-clinician transfer;
- telehealth preparation;
- consent, privacy, accessibility and authorised representative prompts;
- WHS, WorkCover and insurance evidence registers.
