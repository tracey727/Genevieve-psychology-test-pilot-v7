# GENEVIEVE brand and colour control — Irene build

## Approved brand elements

1. Exact GA emblem: metallic-gold outer A, black centred G, short gold horizontal dash, white background and soft-pink glow.
2. Exact tree, infinity and roots image with black structure and pink detail.
3. GENEVIEVE App™ / GENEVIEVE HEALTH™ wordmark used with the approved marks.

The raster masters must not be redrawn, recoloured, cropped, stretched or retouched.

- Deep black and charcoal navigation

- White and softly faded pink surfaces
- Dark professional borders
- Pink accents drawn from the tree-and-roots mark
