## What this demonstration does

This is a working, fictional-data demonstration designed to stop urgent work, client follow-up, staff coverage, reception messages, appointments and handovers from falling between people when the practice is under pressure.

The permanent **Activate Emergency Cover** control demonstrates how the practice could respond when sudden illness, hospital admission, an emergency operation or another urgent event removes a staff member. It:

- identifies affected fictional clients and appointments;
- names a first backup and second backup;
- records acceptance or escalates declined/unanswered work;
- keeps Irene or an authorised delegate in the escalation chain;
- records client-contact outcome and the next appointment or plan;
- prevents closure until required contact outcomes and return-handover evidence are recorded.

The demonstration also contains psychology-practice operational modules for staff coverage and wellbeing, provisional psychologist supervision, client continuity, appointments and telehealth preparation, reception support, referrals, accountable tasks and handovers, immediate safety concerns, incidents, opening/midday/closing checks, WHS psychosocial hazards, WorkCover reminders, insurance registers, reports and audit evidence.

V4 also includes Irene’s complete Director Control Desk, a Documents & Forms centre with her letters and proposal files, role/permission and working-form registers, one-hour break/support alerts and the approved black, metallic-gold, white and soft-pink visual system. The GA emblem, GENEVIEVE App™ / GENEVIEVE HEALTH™ wordmark and locked tree-and-roots mark appear together without redrawing, recolouring, cropping, stretching or retouching.

## Locked safety boundaries

- Fictional data only. Do not enter real client, staff, medical or therapy information.
- GENEVIEVE does not diagnose, calculate a suicide-risk score or declare a person safe.
- GENEVIEVE does not automatically contact clients, family, police, emergency contacts or emergency services.
- Clinical decisions remain with authorised psychologists using Mood & Mind Centre’s approved procedures.
- Reception receives only the minimum operational information required for its role.
- This package is not approved for live clinical use. Production deployment requires security, privacy, clinical governance, WHS, insurance, records-management and legal review.

## How to demonstrate it

1. Extract the ZIP completely.
2. Open `index.html` for a quick demonstration, or run `python -m http.server 8080` in the extracted folder and open `http://localhost:8080`.
3. Begin on the Command Centre and select **Activate Emergency Cover**.
4. Use the fictional default scenario, record first-backup acceptance and show how the event remains visible.
5. Open **Irene: Director Controls** and show her complete permission and responsibility map.
6. Open **Documents & Forms** and show the included files and live working forms.
7. Change the demo role to show Irene, psychologist, provisional psychologist, reception and read-only audit boundaries.
8. Review the Emergency Cover, Client Continuity, Staff & Coverage, Tasks & Handovers, Safety & Incidents and Reports & Evidence pages.

## First tweaking session

The first review with Irene should confirm her real practice terminology, roles, escalation timing, authorised delegates, staff-coverage order, opening/midday/closing checks, reception scripts and which reports she wants. No real client information is needed for that session.
