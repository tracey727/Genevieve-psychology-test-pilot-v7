# Official sources for professional review

Checked in July 2026. Confirm the current version before production use.

- Psychology Board of Australia — Code of conduct for psychologists (effective 1 December 2025):
  https://www.psychologyboard.gov.au/Standards-and-Guidelines/Professional-practice-standards/Code-of-conduct.aspx
- Psychology Board of Australia — Requirements for supervisors:
  https://www.psychologyboard.gov.au/Registration/Supervision/Requirements-for-supervisors.aspx
- Psychology Board of Australia — Professional indemnity insurance:
  https://www.psychologyboard.gov.au/Registration/PII.aspx
- Office of the Australian Information Commissioner — health information and private health service providers:
  https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/your-privacy-rights/health-information
- WorkSafe Queensland — mental health and psychosocial safety:
  https://www.worksafe.qld.gov.au/safety-and-prevention/mental-health
- WorkSafe Queensland — incident notification:
  https://www.worksafe.qld.gov.au/safety-and-prevention/incidents-and-notifications/notify-us-of-an-incident
- WorkCover Queensland — accident insurance and claim/reporting services:
  https://www.workcoverqld.com.au/

These sources inform review fields only. The software does not interpret law or make notification, insurance or clinical decisions.
