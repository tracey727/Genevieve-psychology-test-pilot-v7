# Production readiness checklist

Do not use real identifiable information until all applicable items are approved and tested.

- [ ] Mood & Mind Centre approves all roles, escalation recipients and response times.
- [ ] Clinical lead approves non-attendance, failed-contact and Immediate Safety Concern procedures.
- [ ] Board-approved supervisors approve provisional scope, supervision and competency workflows.
- [ ] Privacy impact assessment and health-information handling review completed.
- [ ] Cybersecurity architecture, penetration testing and incident response approved.
- [ ] Secure authentication, MFA and least-privilege role access implemented.
- [ ] Encrypted production database, transport, backups, recovery and retention implemented.
- [ ] Protected append-only audit log implemented.
- [ ] Practice-management and clinical-record integrations documented and tested.
- [ ] Real emergency/duress arrangements tested; no unauthorised automated contact.
- [ ] WHS consultation and psychosocial risk process completed with workers.
- [ ] WorkCover policy and reporting workflow verified for the business.
- [ ] Professional indemnity and other insurance arrangements verified with insurer/broker.
- [ ] Staff training, supervision and competency records completed.
- [ ] Accessibility, interpreter, minors/guardians and telehealth procedures approved.
- [ ] Business continuity, outage, paper fallback and recovery drill completed.
- [ ] Legal, clinical, privacy, WHS and insurance advisers sign off their areas.
- [ ] User acceptance testing completed on all intended devices.
